usage: randstr [-Lln] [length ...]		

	-L, --upper
		Include uppercase letters (A-Z)
	-l, --lower
		Include lowercase letters (a-z)
	-n, --numbers
		Include numeric digits (0-9)

With no options given, all of the above filters will be active (passing -L, -l, and -n together is the same as passing none).
The length of one or several strings to generate can also be given (if blank, it will be a single string of 8 characters).
